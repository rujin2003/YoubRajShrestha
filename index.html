About me

I am the Managing Director and founder of Hotel Crown Plaza since its inception. Over the past 10 years, I have successfully established Plaza Group as the leading Hotel Chain in Nepal, with multiple properties across the country.

I hold various leadership roles in industry associations and serve as Vice President of Kathmandu District Football Association, executive member of Hotel Association of Nepal, and Director at Himchuli Academy. I am also the Chairman of AYB Foundation, focusing on charitable initiatives and community development.

As a socially responsible hotelier, my contributions have been recognized by national and international organizations. I focus on building libraries, helping rural communities with economic reforms, and providing relief during natural catastrophes.

I am not just your host at Hotel Crown Plaza and Plaza group of hotels but an exceptionally kind-hearted hospitable person.
